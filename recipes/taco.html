Turnpike Tacos

Description

In this tantalizing recipe we will be going over everything involved in making turnpike tacos. I know you must be wondering, and yes this meal involves turning roadkill into some tasty tacos.


Ingredients

  • Propane Torch
  • Hubcap (preferrably without holes)
  • Juicy Road Meat
  • Stormwater from a catch basin for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Skin and debone carcass
  2. Grind Meat into small chunks
  3. Simmer meat in 2 cups of stormwater for 30 minutes
  4. in a seperate hubcap gently warm skin until it has a nice crisp to it
  5. Generously slop meat into freshly warmed "tortillas"
  6. Enjoy!

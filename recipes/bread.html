Bread

Description

If you've been following along with my other recipes and thought about how we're wasting all the bones from our carcass cuisine then you've underestimated our ability to use nearly every piece of these critters. In this recipe we will be going to make bread from all those bones.


Ingredients

  • Campstove
  • Bones
  • Stormwater
  • Grass Seed

Instructions

  1. Grind bones to fine powder
  2. Stir in 1 cup of storm water
  3. Grind grass seeds and add in until mixture has playdough like consistensy
  4. Cook overnight until it is a crunchy husk
  5. Enjoy!
